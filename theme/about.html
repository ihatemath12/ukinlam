About Me

From an energetic and academically strong student in primary school, serving in roles such as class leader and disciplinary prefect, to an aspiring handball athlete, my life has been a diverse and enriching journey. In those early years, I explored different areas of interest, including volleyball, basketball, and harmonica classes, consistently performing well acadically by ranking within the top ten of my class each year.

Transitioning into secondary school, I immersed myself in community service, participating in flag selling and visiting nursing homes to interact with and care for the elderly. I proved my resilience and leadership capabilities during a six-day defense education camp in my second year, where I won the championship in a hazmat suit competition and received an award for being an outstanding student.

My interest in handball sparked when I discovered that the sport required more strength and physical collision than basketball, and less height requirement, making it suitable for someone with my innate strength. Since my third year in secondary school, I took up the mantle of the captain of our school's handball team. I later joined training with the Macau Handball Team and successfully became a member of the Macau Youth Handball Team (U16). I received several awards in different tournaments from 2020 to 2023, and am currently preparing for the Taiwan University Handball Invitational in June 2023.

After years of dedication and growth, my aspiration for university is to study Physical Education in Taiwan. My motivations are twofold. Firstly, Taiwan offers a superior development platform for handball, which would allow me to hone my skills while pursuing my studies, with the eventual goal of bringing these improved techniques back to Macau. Secondly, I aspire to be a physical education teacher, with the aim of promoting handball to a larger audience of students in Macau.

Position on the court

Center back

the center back is a position on the court that is typically played by a versatile and skilled player who can both attack and defend effectively. The center back is positioned in the center of the court, usually slightly behind the halfway line. The main role of the center back is to initiate the team's attack, directing play and setting up scoring opportunities for their teammates. In addition to their offensive duties, the center back is also responsible for organizing the team's defense, communicating with teammates, and blocking shots by the opponent's backcourt players. The center back is considered a key position in handball, as they are often involved in every aspect of the game.

Left back

the left back is a position on the court typically played by a left-handed player. The left back is positioned on the left side of the court, near the opponent's goal area. The main role of the left back is to participate in the team's attack, creating scoring opportunities by passing, shooting, and dribbling the ball. Additionally, the left back is also responsible for defending against the opponent's right back and helping out in defense as needed

Right back

the right back is a position on the court typically played by a right-handed player. The right back is positioned on the right side of the court, near the opponent's goal area. Similar to the left back, the right back's main role is to participate in the team's attack, creating scoring opportunities by passing, shooting, and dribbling the ball. The right back is also responsible for defending against the opponent's left back and helping out in defense as needed.
