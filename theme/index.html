Sports

Basketball

This was the first sport that I seriously trained in during my life. When I was a kid, I would often go to play with my cousin during holidays, and basketball was his favorite sport. Every time he went out, I would plead with him to take me along. In sixth grade of primary school, I met the captain of the high school basketball team at the bridge court, and under his guidance, I joined the basketball school team.

Handball

After playing basketball for a year in junior high school, I was kicked off the school team due to poor grades. However, I didn't stop and chose to join the handball team. Despite never having been exposed to this sport before, I slowly discovered the fun in it and trained hard. I became the team captain in the second year. Finally, in my third year of junior high school, I was fortunate enough to join the Macau youth handball team and won third place in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macau Greater Bay Area Youth Handball Championship in December 2020.

Shot put

I first came into contact with this sport during my physical education class in my first year of junior high school and learned some basic knowledge about it. In my second year, I participated in the school sports meet shot put competition for the first time and was lucky enough to win the championship and break the record for the joint school sports meet. As I gained more knowledge about this sport, I also started to watch competition videos online in my spare time.
